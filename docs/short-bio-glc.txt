Dr. Giovanni Luca Ciampaglia is a young researcher whose scientific interests
are online collective behavior, social production, and social information
processing. He holds a Laurea (M.Sc. equivalent) in Computer Science from
Sapienza University of Rome, and a Ph.D. in Informatics from the University of
Lugano (Switzerland), obtained under the joint supervision of Luca Maria
Gambardella (IDSIA), Alberto Vancheri (SUPSI), and Paolo Giordano (TU Wien). His
research has been supported by Swiss National Science Foundation and he has been
a visiting scientist at the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology in Zurich. He
is currently based in Zurich, Switzerland.
