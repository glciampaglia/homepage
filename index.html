About Me

I'm a Ph.D. candidate in Informatics, at Università della Svizzera Italiana (also known as University of Lugano), since November 2006.

My research advisor is Luca Maria Gambardella (IDSIA) and I am co-advised by Alberto Vancheri (SUPSI) and Paolo Giordano (Uni Wien)

My research interests are social media and online communities, social information processing, agent-based modeling, complex systems. I've been also working on models of urban growth using agent-based simulations.
