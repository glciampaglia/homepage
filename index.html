About Me

I'm a Ph.D. student @ Faculty of Informatics, Università della Svizzera Italiana, since Nov. '06.

I am a member of MACS-Lab @ Accademia d'Architettura, Università della Svizzera Italiana, a research group involved in the mathematical modelling of complex systems. my research advisor is Luca M. Gambardella, director of "Istituto Dalle Molle di Studi sull'Intelligenza Artificiale".

My research interests are social media and online communities, social information processing, agent-based modeling, complex systems. I've been also working on models of urban growth using agent-based simulations.
