About Me

I'm a Ph.D. candidate in Informatics, at Università della Svizzera Italiana (also known as University of Lugano). Here is a link to my curriculum vitae.

My research advisor is Luca Maria Gambardella (IDSIA), and I am co-advised by both Alberto Vancheri (SUPSI) and Paolo Giordano (Uni Wien)

My research is about user participation to peer-production systems (such as Wikipedia). In general, I am interested in social informatics, social information processing, and the nowadays ubiquitous social media.

I work a lot with agent-based modeling, a simulation paradigm that is well-known in the complex systems community and that is increasingly common in the social sciences too. Not so long ago, I used to work on models of urban growth too.
